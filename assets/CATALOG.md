## 1. PURPOSE

Every shipped asset must be listed here before release.

Runtime asset downloads are forbidden during gameplay. Assets must be local, licensed, and reviewable.

---

## 2. APPROVED SOURCES

- Kenney
- OpenGameArt
- CC0
- Public Domain
- Explicitly licensed original work

Any other source requires review before use.

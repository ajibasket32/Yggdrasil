# PURPOSE

This document defines the canonical lore of the world.

All AI systems, gameplay systems, quests, NPCs, factions, locations, items, monsters, and world events must follow this document.

If AI-generated content conflicts with this document:

The World Bible is always correct.

---

# CANON POLICY

This project is inspired by the YGGDRASIL setting from Overlord.

However:

The game takes place in an alternate timeline.

The game does not use copyrighted story characters as active participants.

Release content must not reference protected characters, guilds, distinctive events, copied terminology, exact plotlines, dialogue, or copyrighted assets from another franchise unless separately licensed and approved.

The title, setting, names, story, factions, creatures, visual identity, and promotional materials require an originality review before public distribution. Inspiration is allowed; implementation must be independently original.

# THE AGE SYSTEM

World history is divided into ages.

---

# AGE OF CREATION

The oldest known age.

Myths claim:

- The world was shaped.
- The first races emerged.
- The first dragons were born.

Very little evidence remains.

---

# AGE OF DRAGONS

Dragon Lords ruled the world.

Dragons were the dominant power.

Kingdoms did not exist.

Many ruins from this era still exist.

---

# AGE OF KINGS

The first large civilizations appeared.

Human kingdoms expanded.

Elven forests flourished.

Dwarven empires reached their peak.

Many legendary artifacts were forged.

---

# AGE OF FALLING STARS

A mysterious catastrophe reshaped the world.

Many kingdoms vanished.

Ancient cities became ruins.

Powerful artifacts were lost.

The true cause remains unknown.

---

# CURRENT AGE

The Age of Adventurers.

Most of the world remains unexplored.

Ancient ruins continue to be discovered.

Political tensions rise.

New powers emerge.

The future is unwritten.
